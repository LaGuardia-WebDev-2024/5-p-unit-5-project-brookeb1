Cats

Cats are very misunderstood animals. Here are some reasons to change your mind about them! Many people characterize cats as mean and scary, but they are actually extremely cute and kind. Cats are very independent and love their owner very much. They can also be funny, but are very loving. If you are more of an active person, there are other cat breeds that are more playful to match your personality. There are also some that are more lazy and sleepy if this fits you!

Cute Cat Pics

Cats are believed to be the only mammals who don’t taste sweetness. Cats are nearsighted, but their peripheral vision and night vision are much better than that of humans. Cats are supposed to have 18 toes (five toes on each front paw; four toes on each back paw). Cats can jump up to six times their length. Cats’ claws all curve downward, which means that they can’t climb down trees head-first. Instead, they have to back down the trunk. Cats’ collarbones don’t connect to their other bones, as these bones are buried in their shoulder muscles. Cats have 230 bones, while humans only have 206. Cats have an extra organ that allows them to taste scents on the air, which is why your cat stares at you with her mouth open from time to time.
